## How do I install CodeWave Studio?

You can install CodeWave Studio on macOS, Windows, or use it as a web application. Follow the installation instructions [here](installation).

## How do I register or log in?

Open the application or visit the [website](https://www.codewavestudio.io/), and follow the prompts to register a new account or log in with your existing credentials.

## How do I sync with my IDE?

Use the CodeWave Studio Connector to synchronize with your preferred IDE. Detailed instructions can be found within the application settings.

## How can I contact support?

For any issues or inquiries, you can contact us at help@codewavestudio.io. We're here to help you with any questions or concerns you may have.
