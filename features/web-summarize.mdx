### How to Use

1. **Add a New Summary**:

   - Click on the "Add new" button on the Summaries page.
   - Enter the URL of the content you want to summarize.
   - Click the "Save" button to add the summary to your list.

2. **Manage Summaries**:

   - **Search for Summaries**: Use the search bar at the top to find specific summaries by their title or URL.
   - **View Summary Details**: Click on a summary's title to view its details.
   - **Edit a Summary**:
     - Click on the "Actions" button next to the summary and select "Edit".
     - Change the URL or title as needed.
     - Click the "Save" button to update the summary.
   - **Delete a Summary**:
     - Click on the "Actions" button next to the summary.
     - Confirm the deletion to remove the summary from your list.

### UI Components

#### Add New Summary

- **URL Field**: Text input for the URL of the content.
- **Save Button**: Button to save the summary.

#### Manage Summaries

- **Search Bar**: Text input for searching summaries.
- **Summary List**: Display of all the summaries.
- **Actions Button**: Dropdown menu with options for editing and deleting summaries.
